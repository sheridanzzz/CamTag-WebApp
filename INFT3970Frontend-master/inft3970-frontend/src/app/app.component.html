Welcome to CamTag; a photo-based mobile tag game that works by taking photos of one another to ‘tag’ them and earn points or eliminate them entirely.
The object is to accumulate points via ‘tagging’ (taking a photo of) a fellow player, and having the highest score, or being the last player at the end of the game.
Each ‘tag’ photo of a player is peer reviewed by the other players through a voting mechanism determining if the image is of the specified player, so please be honest! Players have limited ammunition, therefore it is best to take the clearest photos possible.
CamTag currently has two game modes - Tag and Battle Royale.
Tag: A fixed time limit is set, and at the end of the period the player with the most amount of points is the winner. Tag can be played over the course of 10 minutes to a maximum of 24 hours.
Battle Royale: The host determines a starting point and radius of the game zone, which will then shrink gradually and linearly as the game progresses. A player must remain within this zone in order to play, which will forcefully bring the players together over time. Each player has a single life and a successful tag against them will have them eliminated. Battle Royale can be player within a radius of any size.
Creating and joining a game:
  1. Press create game on the main menu.
  2. Set the game parameters.
  3. Enter your nickname.
  4. Enter a phone number or email address.
  5. Verify your contact method.
  6. Create and join the game.
  7. Share the lobby code to other players.
To join a game, simply press the join button and follow similar steps to enter a lobby code.
